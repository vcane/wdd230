Annual Preston Community Park

Trunk or Treat

The Annual Preston Community Park Trunk or Treat will be held on Saturday, October 30th starting at 6:30 P.M. Prepare your little pirates, superheroes, princesses, and unicorns to collect their year's worth of candy. Come and decorate your trunk with tricks and don't forget the treats! The annual event draws crowds from the surrounding area. Vendors will be be available if you decide you need a warm drink or some other treat. Community sponsors are also preparing games for the kids. A haunted maze will lead unsuspecting victims through the spooky haunted forest.
