Five Day Forecast

Light The Tree Festival

On December 12th, the Fish Haven Activities Committee will hold its annual Light The Tree Festival. The local community center will be decorated with Christmas decorations. The community potluck dinner will begin at 5:30 pm. Please bring the side dish or dessert that you signed up for. The local Pit Masters Club will be providing the smoked ham. Please bring your own drink. The dinner portion of the activity will begin to close down at 7:30 pm.

At precisely 6 pm, a very special guest will show up to hear the Christmas wishes that anyone may have. He will be available from 6 pm - 8 pm. He will have small gifts for all the children age 8 and younger. At 8 pm, he will lead everyone out to the tree for the lighting ceremony. After everyone is gathered, Santa will press the button to light the tree.
