Join Us

Instructions

Please complete the form below to request a membership in the Topeka Chamber of Commerce. Every label marked with a red-dashed cell is required to be completed. The required information is your first name, last name, email, business name, and membership level. When the required cell is filled with valid information, a double-lined green border will appear.

All businesses and individuals that members of the chamber receive the Member-To-Member benefits regardless of membership level. The Member-To-Member benefits provide discounts to members of the chamber. The discounts are set by the business providing the discount. A member must request the discount and present employee identification to prove they are employed by a member of the chamber. Each membership level has besides the Member-To-Member Benefits. The following list provides a description of the benefits of each membership level. Please contact staff at the Chamber of Commerce for more details on the benefits.

  • Gold membership benefits:
    • Cost: $1000/year
    • 5 business days of training for employees per year.
    • 75% discount on tickets to the annual conference.
    • Sponsorships for advertisements.
    • Plus all benefits that the lower membership levels receive.
  • Silver membership benefits:
    • Cost: $400/year
    • 3 business days of training for employees per year.
    • 50% discount on tickets to the annual conference.
    • Plus all benefits that the lower membership levels receive.
  • Bronze membership benefits:
    • Cost: $200/year
    • Free social media advertisements.
    • 25% discount on tickets to the annual conference.
    • Plus all benefits that the lower membership levels receive.
  • Non-profit membership benefits:
    • Cost: $100/year
    • Free social media advertisements.
    • 50% discount on tickets to the annual conference.
    • Free social media advertisements.
