Site Purpose

The main purpose of Tri-County News is provide reliable and accurate news and information for the Preston, Soda Springs, Fish Haven and the tri-county area. The information provided will include news articles covering city and county events throughout the area, pages dedicated to each city, a storm center, and a gallery page. The goal of the organization is build community bonds by informing our neighbors of local stories that impact lives and providing a medium to advertise local events.

Home Page

The home page will have a current weather story, the photo of the day, and sections for each town. The information sections include breaking news headlines, article titles, and upcoming events scheduled in the town. An image of a prominent location in town will be displayed in each section.

City Pages

Three pages will each be dedicated to Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. Each page will have town-specific weather forecasts, local news, relevant articles, and an event calendar. The pages will also provide information on local attractions for each city. Individuals or groups will be able to advertise events that they are hosting scheduled for each town.

Storm Center

The Storm Center will provide in depth weather stories and educational information regarding the local climate. Important information will be posted on the Storm Center page when severe weather is threatens the region. Closures of schools and cancellations of events due severe weather conditions will be posted in the Storm Center page.

Gallery

The Gallery will provide a medium for the community members to upload images that they capture during everyday activities or during special events. The user will be requested to provide a brief description of the image, location where the image was captured, and other pertient information. In December, a poll will be activated to allow users of the website to vote on their favorite images for the past year. The first, second, and third place winners will receive gift cards from local business owners. The purpose of the contest is to build our community and promote our local photography hobbyists.

Site Domain

The site domain will be www.tricountynews.org. The site domain was searched for on Google Domains and verified that it is available.
