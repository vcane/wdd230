Target Audience

The target audience will primarily be adults. The expected primary users will be people looking for a reliable and accurate information covering their town and surrounding county areas. We also expect to have users with a wide range of experience with technology. Therefore, the website will need to be user friendly for people with little experience with technology. The median age of the three counties is about 36 years old. The median income is approximately $57,000 annually. With this information, we expect most of our users to come from households with adults and young children. We expect that they will be looking for events which are family friendly.

Persona 1: Heather

Heather is a 35 year old married mother of three kids. She is local florist in Preston, ID. Heather is currently trying to expand her business to also cover the communities ofSoda Springs, and Fish Haven. She is very well respected local small business owner who sponsors our contests and advertises on our website. Heather relies on the Tri-County News to provide accurate weather forecasting and information on local events for her family to attend. Her kids are Charlie (11), Susie (9), and Jimmy (7). The kids enjoy the local sports such as Little League and soccer. When she has an opportunity, she enjoys going on hikes and being outdoors with her family. On these outings, she enjoys taking photos of weather phenomenon especially thunderstorms blowing in over mountains.

Persona 2: Jim

Jim is a 28 year old bachelor that enjoys being outdoors more than indoors. He is a construction site manager for a local residential construction company that serves the tri-county area. He recently graduated with a bachelor's degree in construction management. Now he enjoys his extra time since he longer attends college. When he is not at work, Jim can be found out in the local forest or at the local lake. He enjoys backpacking, camping, fishing, and hunting. He relies strongly on accurate weather forecasts. When he plans his activities, he utilizes the forecast to ensure that he is prepared for the weather. He enjoys sharing his outdoor experiences with others and writes a weekly article about the local outdoor activities that are available in the tri-county region.

Scenario 1

Where can someone find information regarding scheduled community events either in a county or city?

Each city page will include a calendar with scheduled events for that community. Also, contact information for the local tourist office will be provided.

Scenario 2

Can I get notifications of severe weather, closures, or cancellations due to weather events?

The Storm Center page will provide information regarding severe weather, closures, or cancellations due to severe weather. If you subscribe with Tri-County News, we can provide severe weather alerts via push notifications based on your zip code.

Scenario 3

My sister and her family will be coming to town for the weekend. What events are happening this weekend? What attractions are there in the area for young children?

Each city page will have a calendar of events. Also, the tourist office for each town often has lists of attractions in their city. The contact information for the tourist office can be found on each city page. Google Maps are embedded into the website. The user can utilize this feature to search for attractions in the area of interest.

Scenario 4

What are the most popular restraurants in each town or the region?

A freelance contributor writes an article every two weeks about her critiques of local restraurants. A user can find this article on the home page of the website.

Scenario 5

I took several pictures this weekend of our outing at Bear Lake near Fish Haven. Can I upload those photos?

Photos can be uploaded to the Gallery page of the website. A simple form will need to be completed to provide some basic contact information.
